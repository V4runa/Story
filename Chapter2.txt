The land of ruins laid eastwards from the general's owns territory. It was the nearest settlement and he figured it would be easiest to contest.

He gathered his most skilled warriors and formed several units with them as the leaders. 

There was a ranged unit, a comando unit, and close quaters unit, and a support unit. 

Soon the troops and the general had ready themselves for battle and began their march towards the defenceless land of ruin.
