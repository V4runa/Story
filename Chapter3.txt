They traveled as far as they could eastwards but nightfall was afoot and so they proceeded to make camp for the evening. 

The general was hungry for battle and in his drunken stuppor he decided to make a speech to boost the military morale. 

"Tonight, we rest as mortals. Tomorrow we raise as gods of death upon the fools of ruin. 

Let us join in drink to celebrate our ascension brothers!"

The warriors cheered in support, all but the ranged unit. Something seemed to be bothering them.
